Restify FAQs

    Restify is a platform that connects people who need a place to stay with people who have a spare room or property. Hosts list their space on the platform, and guests can book it for a fee.

    Restify has a number of safety measures in place to protect both guests and hosts, such as verified identity, user reviews, and a secure platform for payment. However, as with any online platform, it's important to take precautions and exercise caution when using Restify.

    Restify offers a variety of properties, including apartments, houses, vacation homes, and even unique spaces like treehouses and yachts.

    To create a listing on Restify, you will need to create an account and provide information about your property, such as its location, type, and amenities. You will also need to upload photos and set a price for your listing.

    To make a reservation on Restify, simply search for properties that meet your criteria, such as location and dates, and then follow the booking process. You will need to provide payment information to complete the reservation.

    Restify's cancellation policy varies depending on the type of property and the host's preferences. Some properties may have a flexible cancellation policy, while others may have a strict policy. It is important to carefully review the cancellation policy before making a reservation.

    You can communicate with the host on Restify through the platform's messaging system. This is a secure way to ask the host questions or share important information before your trip.

    You can pay for a Restify reservation using a credit or debit card through the platform's secure payment system. Payment is processed after the reservation is confirmed.

    If you encounter a problem with the property during your stay, you should contact the host immediately through the Restify platform. If the issue cannot be resolved, Restify's customer support team is available to assist you.

    Yes, after your trip, you will have the opportunity to leave a review for the property and the host on Restify. These reviews help future guests make informed decisions and provide valuable feedback to the host.
